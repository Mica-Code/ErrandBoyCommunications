About Errandboy Communications

Honest phones, honest people.

We started Errandboy Communications to fix a simple problem: too many people in Nigeria were getting burned buying used phones from strangers online. We test everything, grade honestly, and stand behind what we sell.

Our Story

Built on the same trust that runs through everything we do.

Errandboy Communications grew out of Errandboy Liaison Services' commitment to reliability — the same team that helps people manage property and errands back home now brings that same discipline to gadgets. We know that for many customers, a phone isn't just a device — it's a lifeline to work, family, and opportunity, so we treat every sale with the seriousness it deserves.

Every phone that reaches our shelves has passed through a hands-on inspection process, whether it's a budget-friendly classic or the latest flagship. No inflated grades, no hidden faults — what you see on WhatsApp is exactly what you get.

Our Framework

How a phone earns a spot in our catalogue

A straightforward, four-step process designed to protect you, the buyer.

1

Source

We source directly from vetted US suppliers and trade-in partners — never from unverified street sellers.

2

Test & Grade

Every unit goes through a 16-point check covering battery health, screen, cameras, buttons, ports and network compatibility, then is assigned an honest condition grade.

3

List Honestly

Storage, grade and origin are all displayed clearly in our catalogue, with final pricing confirmed directly on WhatsApp — no surprises when you arrive to inspect.

4

Deliver & Support

Pick up in Ago-Okota, Lagos, or have it delivered nationwide. Our WhatsApp line stays open for support after your purchase.
